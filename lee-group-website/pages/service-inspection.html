Inspection Services

Lee Group is your trusted partner in Inspection Services, guaranteeing integrity, safety, and compliance across all petroleum operations. Our certified inspectors use state-of-the-art equipment to deliver accurate, reliable assessments.

1 Tubular Inspection Services

Comprehensive drill pipe, casing, and tubing inspection services ensuring structural integrity and operational safety. Our inspectors are certified to international standards.

2 Rig Parts and Handling Tools Inspection

Meticulous examination of rig components and handling tools for optimal functionality. We ensure every piece of equipment meets rigorous performance standards.

3 Lifting Equipment Inspection and Proof Load Testing

Industry-standard compliance testing for all lifting equipment. Our proof load testing ensures safety margins are met and maintained across all operations.

4 Dropped Object Surveying

Systematic hazard identification and preventive measures to eliminate dropped object risks. Our surveys identify potential hazards before they become incidents.

5 NDT Inspection Services

Non-Destructive Testing for structural integrity verification. We employ multiple NDT methods including ultrasonic, magnetic particle, dye penetrant, and radiographic testing.

6 Lifting Operation Supervision

Expert supervision from planning to execution of all lifting operations. Our experienced supervisors ensure compliance with safety standards throughout every lift.

7 Training Services

Comprehensive training programs equipping teams with skills for safe and efficient operations. We offer customized training modules tailored to specific operational needs.
