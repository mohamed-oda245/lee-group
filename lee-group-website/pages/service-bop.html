Blowout Preventers (BOP)

Lee Group provides a complete BOP suite critical for well control operations. Our blowout prevention equipment is maintained to the highest standards, ensuring the safety of personnel and assets during drilling operations.

🔵

Annular BOP

Seals the annular space between the drill string and wellbore. Our annular BOPs provide reliable sealing around any tubular size or even an open hole.

🔴

RAM BOP

Robust rams that seal the wellbore in various conditions. Available in pipe ram, blind ram, and shear ram configurations for complete well control capability.

🔄

Rotating BOP

Provides continuous wellbore access while maintaining a secure pressure barrier. Essential for managed pressure drilling and underbalanced operations.

🎛️

BOP Control Unit

Real-time monitoring and swift response systems for BOP operations. Our advanced control units ensure rapid actuation when safety demands it most.

⚠️

Kill Manifold

Enables safe injection of heavy fluids to control well pressure. Our kill manifolds are engineered for high-pressure, high-reliability performance.

🔧

Choke Manifold

Controlled fluid flow during well control operations. Precision choke manifolds allow operators to manage wellbore pressure safely and effectively.

🔗

Flexible Choke Kill Line

Flexible high-pressure connection between BOP and choke/kill manifold. Provides reliable flow paths while accommodating rig movement and vessel heave.
