Wireline Services

Lee Group is a leader in Wireline Services, delivering precision solutions that play a pivotal role in optimizing well performance. Our comprehensive wireline capabilities cover the full spectrum of well evaluation, completion, and intervention operations.

📊

Logging Services

Resistivity, porosity, gamma ray, and sonic logging for comprehensive reservoir evaluation. Our advanced logging tools provide accurate formation data for informed decision-making.

💥

Perforating

Advanced perforating tools designed to enhance reservoir connectivity. We utilize cutting-edge technology to create optimal flow channels between the wellbore and formation.

📈

Production Logging

Fluid velocity, temperature, and pressure measurement along the wellbore. Our production logging services help optimize well performance and identify production issues.

🔗

Slickline Services

Versatile and cost-effective well intervention solutions. Slickline operations provide efficient tool deployment for maintenance, gauge checks, and equipment installation.

🔍

Cased Hole Services

Comprehensive inspection and evaluation of well casing integrity. Our cased hole services ensure the structural soundness and operational safety of completed wells.

⚡

Electric Line Services

Setting and retrieving tools, conducting downhole surveys, and monitoring reservoir conditions. Electric line provides real-time data transmission capabilities.
