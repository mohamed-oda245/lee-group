About Us

Our Story

At Lee Group For Petroleum Services, we are a reputable and leading provider of innovative petroleum services, committed to delivering exceptional solutions to the global energy industry. With an unwavering focus on technology, safety, and sustainability, we strive to set new standards and exceed customer expectations.

Our team of highly skilled professionals possesses unparalleled expertise in various areas of the petroleum sector. We offer an extensive range of services, including Wireline, Well Control, and Inspection Services.

As a company, we firmly believe in the power of innovation. We continuously invest in research and development to stay at the forefront of technological advancements, allowing us to provide our clients with cutting-edge solutions that enhance operational efficiency, maximize production, and reduce environmental impact.

Founded in November 2022 and headquartered in New Cairo, Egypt, Lee Group has rapidly established itself as a trusted partner across Egypt, Libya, and the UAE, securing exclusive agency agreements with globally recognized manufacturers across the United States, UAE, China, Spain, India, and Egypt.
